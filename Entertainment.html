Video games should not replace what you do in your life, video games are there for entertainment purposes only but that doesn't mean it can't help you mentally prepare in the real world. In the words of one of our teachers "video games and movies allow our inner savagery to escape, thats why we enjoy it so much". Many people have the understanding that videogames are horrible for your health, but in reality its not, everything is in your control. You can be the most sweatiest nerd gamer in the world and still be the most healthiest person in the planet, its your choice how you live your life, everything is in your control.
